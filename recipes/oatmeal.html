A warm, comforting bowl of oatmeal. Each spoonful is hearty and soothing, with a creamy texture thats satisfying. There's a delightful balance of sweetness and a hint of warmth from the cinnamon. Perfect for nourishing start to your morning.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup water
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • 1/2 cup quick-cooking oats
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 tablespoon piloncillo, or to taste
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Bring water and cinnamon stick to a boil in a saucepan. Reduce heat and simmer for 5 minutes.
  2. Add oats and cook for 1 minute. Add milk and cook until thickened, 1 to 2 minutes. Remove from heat. Stir in piloncillo, cinnamon, and vanilla.
